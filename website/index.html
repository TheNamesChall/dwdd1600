This Site:

This website is designed to showcase the works of its creator, Caeden Hall, and serve as a portfolio of sorts. Hosted here are various different pieces of art created by Caeden Hall, ranging from 2D as well as 3D works, including the programs that were used to create them.

Mediums Used:

I use a variety of different tools and mediums to make my art, but here are the primary ones I know how to use.

  • Krita
  • Photoshop
  • Adobe After Effects
  • Davinci Resolve
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Indesign
  • Figma
  • Blender
  • Cinema 4D

Out of the programs that I use, these are the ones that I am the most familiar with.

  1. Krita
  2. Adobe Illustrator
  3. Davinci Resolve
  4. Blender

Here is a rundown of the programs listed above and how I use them.

Krita
This is my main art program. It is somewhat similar to photoshop but is more focused on illustration and art than image editing. It is also significantly more stable and less resource intensive than photoshop in my expereince so that is a part of why I use it so often.
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe Illustrator is my main vector based software. I use it to make scalable assets for my designs.
Davinci Resolve
Resolve could honestly be replaced with Adobe After Effects as they're both video editing programs, but I've been using Resolve for longer so it's the one that I'm the most comfortable with.
Blender
Blender is the main program that I use for 3D modelling. While I do know how to use Cinema 4D as well as a little bit of Maya, both of those are very expensive as well as not open source, so I stick with Blender for the most part.
