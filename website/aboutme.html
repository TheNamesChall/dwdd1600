About Me:

Hi! As you may have gleaned from the name of the site, my name is Caeden Hall and I've been making art as a hobby for about as long as I can remember. I've done a small amount of commission work with clients and have some professional education regarding various forms of art.

Most of my work is digital, and that's what I'll be showcasing on this website, but I do have a bit of experience with charcoals and pencils as well.

Experience:

I have four years of professional education on graphic design and many more years of casual drawing experience. I've done a handful of illustration commissions on the side. I have also done a small amount of work making character reference sheets for the illustrative elements of the Pleasant Grove Rock Opera.
